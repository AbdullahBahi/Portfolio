Long-Short Strategy

With Extracted Latent Factors

Platform

Upwork

Date

June 2023

Role

Strategy Design, Research and Implementation

Extending the classical version of Long-Short Strategy which relied on known market factors such as (Size, Momentum, etc..) to create portfolios, this new version relies on constructing non-observable factor portfolios using PCA and Auto-Encoders.

Uses Hierarchical Clustering portfolio optimization (HRP, HERC, NCO) instead of the classic mean-variance optimization to allocate capital across different portfolios.
